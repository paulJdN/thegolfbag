CLUB TEST & REVIEW

Test are made at the practice range and on the course. First : clubs are hitted around 100 to 200 times in order to deliver a good analysis. We use the Toptracer and ShotVision technology to get a good aproximation of the capcity of each club. Second : we go on the course to test it in real conditions. Finally, we add our own expertise and feeling about the club. All of this, numbers and franc review, are gathered in a short review on this website. Don't hesitate to contact us if you want to join the team and become a tester !
